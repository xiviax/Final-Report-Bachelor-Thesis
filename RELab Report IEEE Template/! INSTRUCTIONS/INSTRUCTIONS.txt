How to create a RELab report based on the IEEE template: 
========================================================

1) Update your project description, declaration of originality and pictures in the folder 'Preamble'.
2) Open 'Preamble.tex' in the folder 'Preamble'. Update your information, project picture and portrait picture.
3) Build 'Preamble.pdf'.
4) In the main template folder, open 'My Report.tex'. Update title and author information.
5) Add content of your report and to the appendix 'Appendix.tex'.
6) Build 'My Report.pdf'.
